Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 16, 2026

CLVRCOST ("we", "our", or "us") is a restaurant purchasing and food cost management application developed by Mahmoud Hobloss. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using CLVRCOST, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, collected when you sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In.
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account to manage your data securely in our cloud backend.
  • App usage data: Information about how you interact with the app (such as features used and screens visited) to help us improve the product.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Technical information about app errors and crashes to help us identify and fix issues.
  • Business data you enter: Supplier information, purchase orders, ingredients, recipes, and menu items that you create within the app. This is your operational data — it belongs to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve CLVRCOST:

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • To sync your data across your devices via our cloud backend
  • To ensure the security and integrity of your data
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance
  • To communicate with you about your account if necessary

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell your data, and we do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

CLVRCOST uses the following third-party services to deliver its functionality:

  • Supabase — our cloud database and authentication provider. Your account data and business data are stored on Supabase servers. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. Learn more at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Apple Sign In — used to authenticate users via their Apple ID. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Google Sign In — used to authenticate users via their Google account. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework, and optionally synced to Supabase cloud servers when you are connected to the internet. We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS) and row-level security policies on our database to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Diagnostic and crash data is retained for up to 90 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all data you have entered in the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can edit or update your information within the app.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app under Settings → Delete Account. This removes your data from both your device and our cloud servers.
  • Export: You may request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Arab Emirates, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws including GDPR or UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection.

7. Children's Privacy

CLVRCOST is a professional business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Your continued use of CLVRCOST after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
